What it does

Flamingo bridges SchemeLESS and Bootstrap.

It gives you better default button, link and alert colors, all based on the seed color you provide.

Looking for even more color for your Bootstrap site? Try the 1pxdeep theme.

How to use it

Just include scheme.less and flamingo.less after your other LESS files.

Then, edit @seed-color in flamingo.less with your desired color.
